Top Navigation

Gilded's top toolbar uses two floating Liquid Glass pill buttons — not a bar. The left pill holds a single action (e.g. bell); the right pill holds Profile + More. The page title sits absolutely centred between them, in DM Sans SemiBold 17px with −0.43px tracking.
