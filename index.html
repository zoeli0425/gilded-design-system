Brand Guidelines

How Gilded looks, sounds, and feels — for marketing, partnerships, and anyone communicating on behalf of the brand. For product UI tokens, see the Colours page.

Brand Essence

Gilded helps people see their financial future clearly. The brand is calm, warm, and quietly confident — precious but never flashy. Think heirloom, not jackpot: we speak to long-term wealth, patience, and craft.

Core Palette

The marketing palette is a distilled version of the product token set. Gold leads, ink and paper carry, olive and lavender support. Click a value to copy.

Ratio guidance: roughly 60% Paper, 25% Ink, 10% Gold, 5% Olive/Lavender. Gold loses its value when it is everywhere — use it to reward attention, not to demand it.

Typography
Your future, gilded.
Crimson Text — headlines & display. Serif warmth for big statements. Regular weight only; tighten tracking on large sizes.
Plan with clarity. Gilded tracks your net worth and projects what retirement really looks like.
DM Sans — body copy, UI, captions in marketing materials. Medium for emphasis, Regular for prose.
NET WORTH · $1,400,000 · +3.1%
Roboto Mono — numbers, data labels, eyebrows. Always paired with data, never for prose.
Voice & Tone
Principle 1
Plain numbers
Say “$2,700 saved” — not “crushing it”
Principle 2
Long view
Decades, not days. Never day-trade energy
Principle 3
Calm candour
Honest about trade-offs, no fear-mongering
Do & Don’t
Do
  • Set the wordmark in Crimson Text, generous whitespace around it
  • Lead with real numbers and honest projections
  • Use gold as a highlight against Paper or Ink
  • Pair serif headlines with DM Sans body copy
Don’t
  • Add gradients, bevels, or metallic effects to the wordmark
  • Use urgency language — “act now”, “don’t miss out”
  • Set large blocks of text in gold or on gold
  • Use Roboto Mono for sentences or headlines
